Headquarters

Blockbuster was headquartred in Fort Laureldale FL in 1987, however in 1996 the Headquarters were moved to Dallas, TX.

Did You Know?

Blockbuster had a chance to buy Netflix in the year 2000 for around $50 million dollars, but they declined. This is considered one of the worst moves in business histories.

Did you also know?

There is only one Blockbuster left in the entire USA, the location is in Bend, Oregon. The last 2 locations in Alaska shutdown nearly a year ago. According to records, Blockbuster ceased operations in Nov, 2013.

End Of Blockbuster!

Due to changing market trends and failure to innovate eventually led to Blockbuster's demise. In 2011 Dish Network bought Blockbuster for $320 Million
